# Commercial Usage Policy

This document clarifies what constitutes commercial use of
Terraform-Guardrail.

## Allowed (No Permission Required)

- Internal use within an organization
- Use in CI/CD pipelines for infrastructure validation
- Modifying the source code for internal needs
- Contributing enhancements or fixes back to the project
- Using Terraform-Guardrail in non-commercial education or research

## Restricted (Permission Required)

The following activities are considered **commercial use** and
require explicit written permission:

- Offering Terraform-Guardrail as a paid product or service
- Providing Terraform-Guardrail as part of a SaaS or managed platform
- Embedding Terraform-Guardrail into commercial products
- Selling subscriptions, licenses, or paid support specifically
  for Terraform-Guardrail
- Redistributing Terraform-Guardrail in a monetized offering

## Requesting Commercial Rights

If you wish to use Terraform-Guardrail commercially, please reach out
to discuss licensing terms.

Commercial licensing options may include:
- Enterprise licenses
- OEM agreements
- Managed service rights
- Support and SLA contracts

## Why this policy exists

Terraform-Guardrail is designed to remain:
- Open and accessible
- Community-driven
- Sustainable for long-term development

Restricting commercial usage ensures continued investment,
innovation, and enterprise-grade support.
